About Me

Hi my name is T.M. Carillo. It stands for Talmage Mark. I am 22 years old and I am a senior transfer student at UC Berkeley studying Data Science. I started my college career in the summer of 2019 after graduating high school at Diablo Valley College in Pleasant Hill, Ca. I was originally a CS major but when I initially saw the math classes, I ended up switching into accounting. I finished my AA in accounting and economics in 2021 but, I ended up taking more math classes and 2 CS classes for fun which reignited my interest in tech.
